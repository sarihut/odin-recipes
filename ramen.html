Hikers Ramen

Easy and fast ramen recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of ramen
  • 1 pack of tuna
  • vegetable soup mix (dried vegetables)

Steps

  1. Boil 600 ml water.
  2. Add dried vegetables to the boiling water and boil about 5 minutes.
  3. Add ramen soup mix and ramen to the boiling water.
  4. Boil ramen 3-5 minutes depending what kind of ramen you are using. Check the cooking time from the ramen packet.
  5. When the ramen and vegetables are ready add tuna to the ramen soup.
  6. Enjoy your meal!
