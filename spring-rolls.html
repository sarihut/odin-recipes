Hikers Ramen

Fresh spring rolls recipe.

Ingredients

  • Rice paper
  • Thin rice noodles
  • Butter lettuce
  • Red cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Cucumber
  • Bell pepper
  • Cilantro
  • Red onion
  • Surimi
  • Shrimps

Steps

  1. Start by preparing all your ingredients. Cut vegetables to thin slices.
  2. Place a few pieces of lettuce about one-third from the bottom of the circular wrapper. The width of your fillings will determine the width of the spring roll. You want to leave a couple inches open on the two sides for wrapping purposes.
  3. Then, add the remaining ingredients. The exact order doesnâ€™t matter much. Just pile them on top, making sure to take up the proper width.
  4. Gently pull the bottom of the wrapper up and over the lower portion of the filling. Try to keep the fillings compact as you roll upwards, just until the fillings are enveloped.
  5. Pull each side over to enclose the filling. Continue wrapping upward until your spring roll is fully wrapped!
