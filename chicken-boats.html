Garlic-chicken lettuce tortillas

Tortilla recipe with an Asian twist.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons chili garlic sauce
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons oil
  • 400 g ground chicken
  • 2 shallots, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves
  • ground black pepper
  • lettuce leaves
  • chopped peanuts
  • Stand 'n stuff mini tortillas

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, lime juice, fish sauce, brown sugar, and chili garlic sauce.
  2. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the ground chicken, shallots, and bell pepper and cook until the chicken is browned and completely cooked through, 3-5 minutes. Stir in the garlic and ginger and cook until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the soy sauce mixture and cook until it is slightly reduced. Remove from the heat. Stir in the cilantro and season with pepper.
  4. Heat the tortillas in the microwave and add lettuce leaves in to them.
  5. Add chicken mixture to the tortillas and add peanuts.
